About Elliot Ayertey Nuertey

Elliot Nuertey has over half a decade of experience as a multimedia journalist. As a student, he begun his journey as a journalist from the University of Ghana where he volunteered as a student journalist at Radio Univers 105.7FM. At Univers 105.7FM, he studied broadcast journalism, photo journalism and online journalism. His bylines are in several international outlets including Africanews, Mynigeria, Modernghana, Scoopernews, Dailygraphic, Asaase Radio, Myjoyonline, Citinewsroom, Scoopernews, MX24, Operanews and GhanaWeb.He is currently the Community Manager for Ghana's leading digital news portal, GhanaWeb, and a Web Manager for Africa's leading YouTube MCN Agency, Age Africa Agency.He previous worked with Asaase Radio, Ghana Armed Forces Radio, Scooper News and Opera News.
